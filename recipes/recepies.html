CAMPFIRE-QUESO

For a less indulgent, vegan take, try the stir-together salsa instead. It’s got everything but the meat and dairy.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 8 ounces bulk italian sausage

Steps

  1. Heat a 10-inch cast-iron skillet on a grill grate over a fire. You can also preheat an outdoor grill to medium-high heat (375 degrees F to 400 degrees F (190 degrees C to 200 degrees C)) .
  2. Add beef, sausage, onion, bell pepper, and jalapeño to the skillet. Cook, stirring to break up lumps, until meat is browned, about 8 minutes.
  3. Uncover skillet; continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until cheese is melted and bubbly, 2 minutes more.
  4. Top with avocado, sour cream, and cilantro. Serve with tortilla chips.

Milkshakes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 cups ice cubs

Directions

  1. Place 2 pounds of the strawberries and water in a blender cup and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour strawberry puree into a large pitcher. Add sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla. Stir until well incorporated. Taste; add sugar, a little at a time, as needed.
  3. Add ice cubes and stir. Gently stir in remaining 1/2 pound diced strawberries. Chill until ready to serve.
